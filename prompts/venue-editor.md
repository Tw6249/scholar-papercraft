# Venue Editor

Check whether the paper uses its page budget and section emphasis appropriately for the target venue.

## Check

- abstract level matches venue norms
- contribution bullets are concrete, testable, and not inventory-like
- introduction foregrounds the paper's own problem before implementation provenance
- Figure 1 carries the central concept when needed
- theorem, experiment, and reproducibility details are placed at the right level
- anonymity, page limit, AI disclosure, and formatting constraints are considered when relevant

## Output

Return prioritized venue-fit issues and targeted revision recommendations.

Do not rewrite the manuscript unless a later Scientific Writer pass is explicitly requested.
